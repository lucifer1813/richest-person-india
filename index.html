TOP 10 RICHEST PERSONS IN INDIA

1. Mukesh Ambani

Net Worth:

Over $90 billion

Description:

Mukesh Ambani is the chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, one of India's most prominent conglomerates. His wealth comes from a wide range of businesses, including petrochemicals, refining, oil, telecommunications (Jio), and retail (Reliance Retail).

2.Gautam Adani:

Net Worth:

Over $75 billion

Description:

Gautam Adani is the founder and chairman of the Adani Group. His fortune is rooted in various sectors, including ports, logistics, power generation, and infrastructure development.

3.Shiv Nadar:

Net Worth:

Over $30 billion

Description:

Shiv Nadar is the founder of HCL Technologies, a leading IT services and consulting company. His wealth comes from the growth and success of HCL in the technology sector.

4.Radhakishan Damani:

Net Worth:

Over $20 billion

Description:

Radhakishan Damani is the owner of Avenue Supermarts, which operates the popular retail chain DMart. His wealth is tied to the retail industry.

5.Cyrus Poonawalla:

Net Worth:

Over $20 billion

Description:

Cyrus Poonawalla is the founder of the Serum Institute of India, one of the world's largest vaccine manufacturers. His wealth is closely associated with the healthcare and pharmaceutical sector.

6.Lakshmi Mittal:

Net Worth:

Over $15 billion

Description:

Lakshmi Mittal is the chairman and CEO of ArcelorMittal, a global steel and mining company. His wealth is derived from the steel industry.

7.Savitri Jindal and family:

Net Worth:

Over $15 billion

Description:

The Jindal family is part of the O.P. Jindal Group, which has interests in steel, power, and infrastructure. Their wealth is tied to these industries.

8.Pallonji Mistry:

Net Worth:

Over $15 billion

Description:

Pallonji Mistry is a major shareholder in Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group, a conglomerate with diverse business interest.

9.Uday Kotak:

Net Worth:

Over $15 billion

Description:

Uday Kotak is the founder and CEO of Kotak Mahindra Bank, one of India's leading private sector banks. His wealth is connected to the banking and financial sector.

10.Lakshmi Narayan Mittal:

Net Worth:

Over $15 billion

Description:

Lakshmi Narayan Mittal, not to be confused with Mukesh Ambani, is another prominent figure in the steel industry, serving as the chairman and CEO of ArcelorMittal.
